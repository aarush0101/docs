---
title: "How to Contact Modmail for Support"
description: "Open a Modmail ticket in the NetherGames Discord server to ask staff a question or get help with a general issue not covered in the Help Center."
---

Modmail is the primary way to reach NetherGames staff through our [Discord server](https://ngmc.co/d). It's best suited for general questions directed at staff. If you have an account-related issue — such as a billing problem or a formal appeal — please use our live chat support at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) instead.

<Warning>
  Before opening a ticket, check whether your question is already answered in this Help Center. Searching first saves you time and keeps our support queue clear for issues that genuinely need staff attention.
</Warning>

## Creating a Modmail ticket

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Discord DMs from server members">
    Make sure your Discord privacy settings allow direct messages from members of the NetherGames server. See [Discord's support article](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/217916488) for instructions on how to do this.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the #support channel">
    In the NetherGames Discord server, navigate to the **#support** channel under the **Official** category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Message the Modmail bot">
    Click on the avatar of the Modmail bot, then select **Message** to open a direct message thread.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your question">
    Describe your issue clearly and in full from the start — don't just say "hello" and wait for a reply. The more detail you provide upfront, the faster staff can help you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for a staff response">
    A staff member will review your ticket and reply as soon as possible. Please be patient.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Things to keep in mind

- **Don't spam tickets.** Sending multiple tickets about the same issue clogs the system and may result in a punishment.
- **Be respectful.** Staff can see everything you send in a ticket. Use proper manners at all times.
- **You may be added to a ticket you didn't create.** Staff may add you to an existing ticket if it helps resolve your issue more efficiently.
