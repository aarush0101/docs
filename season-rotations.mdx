Several parts of the NetherGames network run on season-based cycles that periodically reset progress. These resets are designed to keep competition fair and give newer players a more level playing field when they join.

<Info>
All dates are approximate and subject to change. Confirmed dates are announced in the [NetherGames Discord server](https://ngmc.co/discord).
</Info>

## Factions

**Season cycle:** approximately every 4 months

**Current season:** started December 2nd, 2024

**Next season:** March 1st, 2025

<Tip>
Free kits are awarded to players around two weeks before the season ends (approximately February 15th) to celebrate the closing of the season.
</Tip>

## Skyblock

**Season cycle:** approximately every 12 months

**Current season:** started December 2nd, 2023

**Next season:** To be announced

## Credits & Tiers

**Season cycle:** every 12 months

**Current season:** started July 1st, 2025

**Next season:** July 1st, 2026

Find out more about the credits and tiers system at [ngmc.co/tiers](https://ngmc.co/tiers).

## Map rotations

Maps cycle across the year on the following schedule:

| Rotation | Dates |
|----------|-------|
| Rotation 1 | January 21st – March 31st |
| Rotation 2 | April 1st – June 30th |
| Rotation 3 | July 1st – October 14th |
| Rotation 3 + Spooky Season maps | October 15th – November 14th |
| Rotation 4 + Winter Holiday maps | December 1st – January 20th |

You can view all currently active maps at [ngmc.co/maps](https://ngmc.co/maps).
