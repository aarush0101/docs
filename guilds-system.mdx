---

Guilds bring players together to compete as a team, track collective progress, and build a community identity on NetherGames. As your guild earns XP through wins, it levels up and unlocks new cosmetic features like coloured tags.

## Creating a guild

<Warning>
You must have a Legend rank or higher to create a guild.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Social Menu">
    In any lobby, tap or click the Social Menu item in your hotbar (second-to-last slot).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Guilds">
    Click **Guilds** in the Social Menu. If you are not already in a guild, a **Create Guild** option will appear.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your guild">
    Enter a name for your guild. Guild names cannot exceed 20 characters and must not contain inappropriate language.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Inviting players

Guild leaders and officers can invite players to the guild.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Social Menu and go to Guilds">
    Tap the Social Menu item in your hotbar, then click **Guilds**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite a player">
    Click **Invite players**, then select an online player from the dropdown list.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can also hit another player with the Social Menu item — an option to invite them to your guild will appear directly.

## Managing members and roles

To promote members, demote officers, transfer leadership, or kick players:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Guild Members">
    In the Guilds menu (accessed via the Social Menu), click **Guild Members**. A list of everyone in the guild appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a member">
    Click on a player's name to see the available actions for them, such as promoting, demoting, or kicking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You can only transfer guild leadership to a player who holds a Legend rank. As guild leader, you can also manage members and transfer leadership through the [online portal](https://portal.nethergames.org).
</Note>

## Earning guild XP

Your guild earns XP whenever you win a match in specific game modes:

| Game mode | XP per win |
|-----------|-----------|
| Bedwars | 10 XP |
| Conquests | 10 XP |
| SkyWars | 10 XP |
| Survival Games | 10 XP |
| The Bridge | 10 XP |
| Bedwars Duels | 3 XP |
| Duels (1v1) | 3 XP |

<Warning>
Only wins count toward guild XP. You do not earn any guild XP for losses. Game modes not listed above do not award guild XP.
</Warning>

## Setting a guild MOTD

A guild MOTD (Message of the Day) is displayed to all guild members whenever they join the server. Use it to post announcements or pre-match encouragement.

<Warning>
Your guild must be at least level 100 to use the MOTD feature.
</Warning>

To set a guild MOTD, open the Social Menu, navigate to the **Guilds** menu, and click **Edit MOTD**. Type your message in the input box that appears.

## Setting a guild tag

A guild tag is a short label shown at the end of each guild member's name in the lobby. It is a cosmetic-only feature.

<Warning>
Your guild must be at least level 25 to unlock the guild tag feature.
</Warning>

To set a guild tag, open the Social Menu, navigate to the **Guilds** menu, and click **Change guild tag**. Enter your tag (maximum 8 characters) and select a colour.

Available tag colours unlock as your guild levels up:

| Guild level | Available colours |
|-------------|-------------------|
| 25–49 | Gray |
| 50–149 | Gray, Dark Aqua |
| 150–299 | Gray, Dark Aqua, Yellow |
| 300–499 | Gray, Dark Aqua, Yellow, Dark Green |
| 500–749 | Gray, Dark Aqua, Yellow, Dark Green, Pink |
| 750+ | Gray, Dark Aqua, Yellow, Dark Green, Pink, Gold (orange) |

<Tip>
Guilds with a Titan rank holder as the leader are eligible for a Red guild tag.
</Tip>
