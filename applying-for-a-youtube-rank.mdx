---
title: "How to Apply for a YouTube Rank on NetherGames"
description: "What the YouTube rank includes, channel eligibility requirements, and how to submit your creator application through the NetherGames Portal."
---

The YouTube rank grants content creators a special in-game rank that includes all Legend rank perks, including custom nickname permissions. YouTube rank holders also get permission to post NetherGames-related videos in the **#media** channel on the NetherGames Discord server. When you apply, the team reviews your channel to confirm it meets the requirements below.

<Tip>
  If you plan to feature NetherGames in your videos, you can download brand assets from our media kit at [ngmc.co/media](https://ngmc.co/media).
</Tip>

## Requirements

Your channel must meet **all** of the following conditions:

- Either: your channel has **over 1,000 subscribers** and produces NetherGames-related content
- Or: your channel has **over 5,000 subscribers** and produces Bedrock Edition-related content
- Content must conform to NetherGames' family-friendly guidelines
- At least **20 videos** uploaded on your channel
- An average view count of at least **750** per video
- Your channel follows [YouTube's Terms of Service](https://www.youtube.com/t/terms) (you must be at least 13 years old)
- Your channel produces enough Bedrock Edition-related content
- Your videos are reasonably recent and of a decent length

<Warning>
  Channels that are predominantly focused on YouTube Shorts will not meet the requirements.
</Warning>

Your channel must continue to exist and produce Bedrock Edition content for as long as you hold the rank. YouTube ranks are issued and revoked at NetherGames' discretion, and you will receive an email notification if your application status changes.

## How to apply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the application portal">
    Visit [ngmc.co/yt](https://ngmc.co/yt) to open the NetherGames Portal request page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your Microsoft account">
    Log in with the Microsoft (Xbox) account you use to play Minecraft. If you're already signed in, this step is automatic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Apply for YouTube'">
    Choose **Apply for YouTube** from the list of available request types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your Discord and YouTube accounts">
    Follow the on-screen steps to connect both your Discord account and your YouTube channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete and submit the application">
    Fill out all required fields in the application form and submit. You can check your application status at any time by viewing your submissions at [ngmc.co/yt](https://ngmc.co/yt).
  </Step>
</Steps>

You will receive a response within **7 days** to the email address registered on your Xbox account.

<Warning>
  Do not message staff members asking about the status of a pending application. If more than 7 days have passed without a response, contact us through Modmail on our Discord server at [ngmc.co/d](https://ngmc.co/d).
</Warning>
