---
title: "Requesting a Rollback in Factions or Skyblock"
description: "How to submit a rollback request in Factions or Skyblock after losing items due to a cheater, including required evidence and what to expect."
---

If you lose items or progress in Factions or Skyblock as a result of another player cheating, you can apply for a rollback. Rollback requests are reviewed by the NetherGames team and require you to provide sufficient evidence before your application can be considered. Read the guidance below before you submit.

<Warning>
  You must have video evidence of the incident before submitting a rollback request. Applications without sufficient evidence will not be approved. You should also report the cheating player separately at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) under **Player Report** before applying.
</Warning>

## How to submit a rollback request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Report the cheater first">
    Before applying for a rollback, go to [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) and select **Player Report** to report the player responsible for the incident.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to the request portal">
    Navigate to [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with your Microsoft account">
    Sign in using the Microsoft (Xbox) account you use to play Minecraft. If you are already logged in, you can skip this step.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the rollback type">
    Choose either **Skyblock Rollback** or **Factions Rollback** depending on which game mode you were playing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the application">
    Complete all required fields, including your evidence, and submit the form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After submitting, you will receive a response within 7 days to the email address registered on your Xbox account. You can also check the status of your submission at any time by viewing your applications at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request).

<Warning>
  Do not message staff members to ask about the status of a pending application. If your application has been open for more than 7 days without a response, contact the team through Modmail on the NetherGames Discord server.
</Warning>

## Things to keep in mind

- Your rollback request may be denied if you repeatedly ask staff to review it.
- Your request may be denied if the selected inventory has already been rolled back previously.
- If the team detects attempts to abuse the rollback system, you may be banned from submitting future requests.
