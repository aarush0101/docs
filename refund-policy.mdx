The NetherGames Network ("NetherGames," "we," "us," "our") operates an online store at store.nethergames.org where you can purchase ranks and other products. By using the store, you agree to this policy. When you buy a rank, you are purchasing access to virtual items and features on our Minecraft servers — you are paying NetherGames, not Mojang. We are not affiliated with Mojang or Microsoft Corporation.

<Warning>
  Filing a chargeback or dispute request with your credit/debit card provider or PayPal will result in a permanent ban from all NetherGames services and the removal of your rank.
</Warning>

## What you're purchasing

Ranks give you access to virtual items and cosmetic features on our servers. The privileges and features associated with a rank are subject to change without prior notice for players who already hold it. Purchasing a rank does not guarantee winning games — game outcomes are based on your skill, not your rank or its perks.

Ranks are not pay-to-win, and holding a rank does not exempt you from warnings or punishments issued by our staff.

## Account restrictions on purchases

Purchased ranks are tied to one account only. You may not buy a rank and use it across multiple accounts. Attempting to scam ranks will result in a permanent ban and the removal of your rank.

We reserve the right to terminate purchased ranks if the associated game or privileges no longer exist. We will stop selling a rank before it is phased out, but we offer no guarantees on the ongoing existence of purchased perks. We do, however, do our best to keep all privileges available.

## Refunds and the 5-day window

Once you purchase a rank, you have **5 days** to request a refund or to change the account the rank is applied to. After 5 days, NetherGames is not obligated to provide a refund or transfer, unless otherwise required by consumer law in your jurisdiction.

<Tip>
  Changed your mind or made an accidental purchase? Open a **Billing Issue** request at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) within 5 days and we'll sort it out.
</Tip>

To request a refund or account transfer within the 5-day window:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the request portal">
    Visit [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open a Billing Issue ticket">
    Select the **Billing Issue** option and fill in the details of your purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for a response">
    Our team will review your request and follow up with you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If your account is banned for violating our rules, terms, or policies, you are **not** entitled to a refund — even within the first 5 days. By using our services, you agree to these terms.
</Warning>

## Gifts and transfers

Gifts that have already been claimed are not eligible for a refund, even within the 5-day window. This is to prevent gift scamming.

If you have an active punishment on your account, you must wait for it to expire before transferring any gifts.

Players with a Titan subscription can transfer the remainder of their subscription to another player, provided the Titan subscriber is not currently punished. See our [Managing Your Titan Subscription](/managing-your-titan-subscription) article for instructions.

If you are falsely banned and successfully appeal your ban while holding a Titan subscription, contact our support team to receive an extension for the time you were banned.

## Rank removal and proof of purchase

If your rank has been removed without reason, contact our support team and provide proof of purchase. Without evidence of purchase, your rank will not be re-added — even if we removed it in error.

If you purchased a rank or stats transfer token, there must be sufficient evidence in our system linking your old and new accounts to complete a data transfer. If we cannot establish that link, you may be asked to prove ownership of both accounts.

## Payment methods

We support three payment gateways: Stripe, PayPal, and PaysafeCard.

<Warning>
  We cannot provide refunds for purchases made with PaysafeCard under any circumstances.
</Warning>
