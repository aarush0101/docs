---
title: "Linked Accounts and How Punishments Apply to Them"
description: "What it means for your account to be linked to another on NetherGames, how punishments carry across linked accounts, and why you can't unlink them."
---

On NetherGames, your account can become linked to another player's account or device. When this happens, any punishment issued to one of those accounts also applies to the other. Understanding this is important before you share your device or account with anyone.

## What makes accounts linked

Two accounts become linked when they share the same device or when one player logs into the other's account. NetherGames treats these as associated accounts, which means punishments cross over between them.

<Info>
  This applies whether you share a device with a family member, a friend, or anyone else. If you share your device with a sibling and they break the rules, your account will also be affected.
</Info>

## How punishments apply across linked accounts

If one linked account is banned or muted, the punishment applies to all accounts associated with that device or player — including yours, even if you personally did nothing wrong.

For example:
- You share your device with a sibling. They receive a ban. Your account is also banned.
- You previously gave your login details to another player. They were banned while using your account. That ban applies to your account retroactively, even though you weren't the one playing.

<Warning>
  Sharing your account is **not** recommended. NetherGames is not responsible for who you give access to, and is not required to unlink your account if something goes wrong.
</Warning>

## Can I unlink my account?

No. It is currently not possible to unlink your account from another player or device.

## What if I need to share my device or account?

If sharing is unavoidable, make sure you fully trust the person before doing so. Your account is at risk based on their behaviour. They must also follow the [NetherGames community rules](/terms-of-service) — any punishment they receive will affect your account too.

<Tip>
  If you're unsure about a situation involving linked accounts, contact our support team via Modmail on our [Discord server](https://ngmc.co/d) before sharing access.
</Tip>
