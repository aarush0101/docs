---
title: "Unable to Change Your 'About Me' on the NetherGames Portal"
description: "Find out why your About Me update may be rejected automatically or why editing may be blocked, and how to get help if you're trying to set an acceptable value."
---

If your "About Me" update on the NetherGames Portal isn't saving, it was most likely rejected by an automatic content filter. NetherGames uses external APIs to scan all "About Me" values before they are saved.

The filter automatically rejects content that contains profanity, toxicity, sexually explicit material, threats, or other unwanted content. In most cases, the rejection happens silently without any notification. If you repeatedly attempt to bypass this filter — or succeed in doing so — your account may be banned from editing your "About Me" entirely.

<Tip>
  If you're trying to set a normal, appropriate value and it still won't save, submit a request at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) and choose **Chat with us** to get help from the team.
</Tip>
