---
title: "How to Join the NetherGames Discord Server"
description: "Connect with the NetherGames community on Discord to get support, share feedback, and stay up to date with server news and announcements."
---

The NetherGames Discord server is the central hub for our community. Once you're in, you can access support through Modmail, submit feedback, read announcements, and chat with other players. All you need is a Discord account and a browser.

<Warning>
  You must be at least 13 years old to hold a Discord account. If you don't have an account yet, you'll need to create one before joining. Discord's own guide covers the signup process at [discord.onl/how-to-create-a-discord-account](https://discord.onl/how-to-create-a-discord-account/).
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invite link">
    Go to [ngmc.co/d](https://ngmc.co/d) in your browser. This will take you directly to the NetherGames server invite page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in or create an account">
    If you already have a Discord account, select **Already have an account?** and log in. If you're new to Discord, enter a username to create an account and follow the on-screen prompts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the invite">
    Once you're signed in, confirm that you want to join the NetherGames server. You'll immediately have access to all public channels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read the rules">
    Head to the **#welcome** channel and read through the server rules before chatting. Familiarising yourself with the regulations helps you avoid warnings and keeps the community a welcoming place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  For the best experience, download the Discord app for [Windows, macOS, or Linux](https://discord.com/download), [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.discord&hl=en), or [iOS](https://discordapp.page.link/?link=https%3A%2F%2Fitunes.apple.com%2Fus%2Fapp%2Fdiscord-chat-for-games%2Fid985746746%3FattemptId%3D36d43e8c-aee0-4b57-8e59-a70b0a3df876&utm_source=download&apn=com.discord&isi=985746746&ibi=com.hammerandchisel.discord&sd=Your%20place%20to%20talk%20with%20communities%20and%20friends.&efr=1). The desktop and mobile apps give you push notifications so you never miss an important announcement.
</Tip>
