---
title: "Paying with PaysafeCard on the NetherGames Store"
description: "Buy a PaysafeCard at a local retailer and use it to pay for NetherGames ranks or items without a bank card or online payment account."
---

PaysafeCard lets you pay for items on the NetherGames Store using cash — no bank account or credit card required. You buy a PaysafeCard pin at a physical store near you, then enter it at checkout. It works like a prepaid voucher and is available in many countries worldwide.

## Purchasing with PaysafeCard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a retailer near you">
    Use the [PaysafeCard store finder](https://www.paysafecard.com/en/find-sales-outlet) to locate a shop near you that sells PaysafeCards. Many supermarkets and convenience stores carry them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Buy a PaysafeCard">
    Purchase a PaysafeCard in person. It comes with a 16-digit PIN printed on it, similar to a gift card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start checkout on the NetherGames Store">
    On the [NetherGames Store](https://store.nethergames.org), add the item you want to your cart and proceed to checkout. Select **PaysafeCard** as your payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in your billing details">
    If prompted, enter your billing details to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your PIN on the PaysafeCard site">
    Your browser will redirect you to the PaysafeCard website. Enter your 16-digit PIN to complete the payment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you run into any issues, contact the [NetherGames support team](https://ngmc.co/request) for help.

## Supported countries

PaysafeCard is available in the following countries: Argentina, Australia, Austria, Belgium, Brazil, Bulgaria, Canada, Croatia, Cyprus, the Czech Republic, Denmark, Finland, France, Georgia, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Kuwait, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Mexico, Montenegro, Netherlands, New Zealand, Norway, Peru, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Saudi Arabia, Serbia, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Turkey, the United Arab Emirates, the United Kingdom, the United States, and Uruguay.

<Warning>
  If PaysafeCard is not available in your country, there is no other way to pay with cash on the NetherGames Store.
</Warning>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Are there any extra fees for using PaysafeCard?">
    No. NetherGames covers the transaction fees, so you pay exactly the listed price with no surcharges.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to create a PaysafeCard account?">
    Generally, you only need to create an account if you load more than a certain amount onto your PaysafeCard. The threshold varies by country — check the PaysafeCard website for details specific to your region.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund for a PaysafeCard purchase?">
    No. NetherGames cannot provide refunds for purchases made with PaysafeCard. Please make sure you want the item before completing your purchase.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
