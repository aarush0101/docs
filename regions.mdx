NetherGames operates servers in multiple regions around the world so you can connect to the data centre nearest to you. Using a regional address instead of the default one can reduce your ping and improve your gameplay experience. If you are unsure which region to use, the default address automatically routes you to the nearest available server.

<Info>
The North America address has changed from `na.nethergames.org` to `us.nethergames.org` to better reflect its purpose of serving players in the United States.
</Info>

## How to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Minecraft">
    Launch Minecraft on your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Servers">
    Navigate to the **Servers** tab and create a new server entry.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the server address">
    Type `play.nethergames.org` into the **Server Address** field, or use one of the regional addresses listed below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the server (optional)">
    Give the server a recognisable name, such as "NetherGames US."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect">
    Hit **Play** to connect to the server.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
If you are connecting on a console or mobile device, read the full guide at [support.nethergames.org/connecting-on-mobile-and-console](/connecting-on-mobile-and-console).
</Warning>

## Available regions

### Default

Using `play.nethergames.org` automatically connects you to the nearest data centre. For example, players in the US are routed to the Beauharnois, Canada server, while players in India are routed to the Mumbai server.

```text
play.nethergames.org
```

### US (United States)

Hosted in Beauharnois, Canada. Best for players in or near the United States.

```text
us.nethergames.org
```

### IND (India)

Hosted in central Mumbai, India. Added in 2023 to provide better latency for players in the region.

```text
ind.nethergames.org
```

### AP (Asia Pacific)

Hosted in Singapore. Best for players in the Asia Pacific area.

```text
ap.nethergames.org
```

### EU (Europe)

Hosted in central France. Best for players in European countries.

```text
eu.nethergames.org
```

<Tip>
If you are unsure which region gives you the best connection, use `play.nethergames.org` and let the network route you automatically.
</Tip>
