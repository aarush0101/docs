The Partner Program is a select-entry partnership for popular content creators and teams. Offers are evaluated on a case-by-case basis — there are no fixed numeric thresholds, but the team considers a range of factors when reviewing each application. Partner carries stricter expectations than the YouTube rank, and applying does not guarantee acceptance.

## What the review considers

Your application will be assessed based on the following factors:

- You hold a YouTube rank on NetherGames
- Subscriber and follower count across your platforms
- NetherGames activity and previous punishments on your account
- View count across all videos on your channel
- View count on your latest live stream (YouTube and Twitch)
- Discord server size and moderation quality
- View count on long-form videos compared to Shorts content

<Info>
  NetherGames considers creators and teams from multiple platforms, including Discord servers, YouTube, Twitch, X, and Instagram. YouTube Shorts, Instagram/Meta Reels, and TikToks do not count toward partnership and will result in your application being denied.

  Your account must also comply with the Terms of Service of any platform you use. For example, you must be at least 13 years old to hold a YouTube, Meta, X, or Discord account.
</Info>

Applications will not be considered if your account has excessive warnings or if you have been previously banned or muted for severe offences.

## Partner perks

Partners receive the following benefits:

- Ability to set a custom in-game nickname (rather than a randomly generated one)
- Access to [Titan rank perks](https://store.nethergames.org), including public parties, private games, a 40-player party limit, replays, and an exclusive guild tag
- Permission to post your latest NetherGames video in the **#media** Discord channel, plus access to an exclusive Partner channel to connect with other Partners
- Permission to run official NetherGames giveaways through your channel at no cost to you (giveaways are run at NetherGames' request and discretion)
- Assistance with private games for unique video opportunities (game mode, vanilla items, time of day, and more)
- An exclusive Partner rank both in-game and on Discord

## Guidelines

Partners are not exempt from network or Discord server regulations and are expected to hold themselves to a higher standard as community role models. Review [network regulations](https://ngmc.co/tac) and [Discord server regulations](https://support.nethergames.org/discord-server-regulations.html) to make sure you're familiar with the rules.

## How to apply

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the application portal">
    Visit [ngmc.co/partner](https://ngmc.co/partner) to open the NetherGames Portal request page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your Microsoft account">
    Log in with the Microsoft (Xbox) account you use to play Minecraft. If you're already signed in, this step is automatic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Apply for Partner'">
    Choose **Apply for Partner** from the list of available request types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your Discord and YouTube accounts">
    Follow the on-screen steps to connect both your Discord account and your YouTube channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete and submit the application">
    Fill out all required fields and submit the form. You can check your application status at any time by viewing your submissions at [ngmc.co/partner](https://ngmc.co/partner).
  </Step>
</Steps>

You should receive a response within **14 days** to the email address registered on your Xbox account.

<Warning>
  Do not message staff members asking about the status of a pending application. If more than 30 days have passed without a response, contact us by sending a message to the Modmail bot on the [NetherGames Discord server](https://ngmc.co/d).
</Warning>
