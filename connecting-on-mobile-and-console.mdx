---
title: "Connect to NetherGames on Mobile and Console"
description: "How to add NetherGames to your Minecraft server list on iOS, Android, Xbox, and PlayStation — including the server address and port."
---

NetherGames runs on **Minecraft Bedrock Edition** and is accessible from mobile devices and consoles. The process differs slightly depending on your platform — mobile players can add the server directly, while console players need a companion app to get connected.

## Server details

| Field          | Value                    |
| -------------- | ------------------------ |
| Server address | `play.nethergames.org`   |
| Port           | `19123`                  |

## Joining on mobile (iOS or Android)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch Minecraft">
    Open the Minecraft app on your iOS or Android device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in to your Xbox account">
    If you haven't already, sign in to your Xbox (Microsoft) account from the title screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the server browser">
    Tap **Play** on the main screen, then tap **Servers** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new server">
    Tap **Add Server** in the top-middle of the screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the server details">
    Enter the server address **play.nethergames.org** and leave the port as the default **19123**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the server">
    Tap **Play** at the bottom-left to connect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Joining on console (Xbox or PlayStation)

Console editions of Minecraft Bedrock do not support adding custom servers directly. You'll need the **Bedrock Connect** app on a mobile device connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your console.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Bedrock Connect app">
    On your mobile device, download the Bedrock Connect app from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.bedrockhub.connector.bedrockhub_connector) (Android) or the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/bedrockconnect/id6443529739) (iOS).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to the same network">
    Make sure your mobile device is on the same Wi-Fi network as your console.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start NetherGames in the app">
    Open the Bedrock Connect app and press **Start** on the NetherGames option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join from your console">
    On your console, open Minecraft and go to the **Friends** tab. NetherGames will appear as a joinable session — select it to connect.
  </Step>
</Steps>
