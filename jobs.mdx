NetherGames is a community-driven Minecraft Bedrock server network that has grown from a hobby project started in January 2016 into one of the world's largest Bedrock servers, with over 3.9 million players since late 2017. Most staff members — including owners — are dedicated volunteers who receive no compensation for their work. If you want to contribute to the network, several staff positions are open to applicants.

<Info>
All staff positions require applicants to be at least 13 years old and registered on the [NetherGames Discord server](https://ngmc.co/discord), except for Trainee, which requires applicants to be 14 or older.
</Info>

## Application process

All applications receive a response, whether accepted or denied.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit your application">
    Register your interest by applying at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initial review">
    The Applications Department reviews your responses within one week. Applications that meet requirements or need more detail will be allowed through at this stage.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Background check and shortlisting">
    If your application looks strong, it is shortlisted for review by the People & Culture Department, who will perform background checks and verify any references you provide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interview">
    If you pass the second review, you will be invited to a text or voice interview on Discord. Expect questions covering personal background, Minecraft experience, and the role itself.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Skills test (if applicable)">
    Depending on the position, you may be asked to complete a skills test — for example, a live build test for Builder applicants.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Final decision">
    The Executive Team reviews your interview and test results. If you are a good fit, you will be accepted and asked to sign an agreement form.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for a strong application

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Choose the right position">
    Apply for the role that best matches your skills and interests. Applying to multiple positions just to improve your chances does not work and may result in your applications being blocked.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do not beg for acceptance">
    Avoid asking to be accepted in your application. Instead, demonstrate why you are the right candidate with specific, relevant examples.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Give detailed, concise answers">
    For long-answer questions, go beyond the minimum and add context — but stay focused. Reviewers want to learn about you without spending hours reading one application.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use proper grammar and punctuation">
    Professional writing shows you can meet the standards expected of staff members. Proofread your application several times before submitting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Be truthful">
    Applications containing false information may result in dismissal and a permanent block from reapplying if discovered after acceptance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Write your own application">
    Plagiarism is detected. Do not copy from other applicants — write in your own words.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do not ask about your application status">
    The team reviews applications at their own pace. Repeatedly asking about your status may result in your application being denied.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Learn from previous rejections">
    If a previous application was denied, read the reviewer's feedback carefully and address those points in your next attempt.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Volunteer positions in detail

### Trainee

Trainee is the entry-level in-game moderator position. You must be at least 14 years old to apply.

Accepted applicants enter the Trainee Program, a probation period that lasts from one week up to several months. During this time, you will report players who break server rules and assist other players across Discord and in-game. To complete probation and be promoted to Crew, you need a minimum of 2 accepted reports per day.

You are responsible for having a device capable of taking screenshots and recording video.

[Apply for Trainee](https://ngmc.co/request)

---

### Support Representative

Support Representatives help players who have questions or need assistance through Discord, in-game, and the ModMail support system.

[Apply for Support Representative](https://ngmc.co/request)

### Discord Moderator

<Warning>
You must be a former or current NetherGames staff member to apply for this position.
</Warning>

Discord Moderators ensure that all content on the official Discord server is appropriate and that users follow the Discord Server Regulations.

[Apply for Discord Moderator](https://ngmc.co/request)

---

### Beta Tester

<Warning>
Beta Tester applications close in February 2026.
</Warning>

Beta Testers gain access to the development network to evaluate upcoming features, identify bugs, and assess playability before updates go live. Responsibilities include:

- Finding bugs in upcoming updates
- Discussing potential playability issues

[Apply for Beta Tester](https://ngmc.co/request)

---

### Game Designer

<Warning>
Game Designer applications are closed for the foreseeable future.
</Warning>

Game Designers improve existing game modes and develop new content. Responsibilities include designing and reviewing game modes, analysing community feedback, and organising tournaments.

[Apply for Game Designer](https://ngmc.co/request)

## Paid positions in detail

### Developer

Developers work behind the scenes to patch bugs, maintain existing games, and build new features. Upon applying, you will need to provide your GitHub username and three relevant examples of your work.

Open sub-roles include:

- **Kubernetes Engineer** — Scale Minecraft servers dynamically. Prior Kubernetes experience required; note that Minecraft Bedrock uses UDP traffic.
- **Java Developer** — Work on the internal communication system NetSys and proxy servers globally. Prior experience with the Netty framework required.
- **PHP Developer** — Develop and revamp minigames and MMO-based games. Sufficient knowledge of PocketMine API (v5.0) required.
- **C# Developer** — Build a launcher and DLL injector. Sufficient C# experience required.
- **Frontend Developer** — Engineer web interfaces. Prior frontend experience required.

Developers are hired per project as independent contractors and must sign an NDA upon acceptance. NetherGames does not provide training or equipment.

[Apply for Developer](https://ngmc.co/request)

---

### Builder

Builders create server lobbies, game lobbies, and custom maps. Before being accepted, you will undertake a live trial-building test where you create something based on a given theme, using a world editing tool, without assistance from others. You need to provide three examples of your builds in your application.

[Apply for Builder](https://ngmc.co/request)
