NetherGames understands that many players — especially those on PC — use modifications to improve their experience. However, some third-party modifications provide an unfair advantage over other players and undermine fair play. This page lists what is and isn't allowed so you know exactly where the line is.

<Info>
  The general rule is that any modification you use while playing on NetherGames must not provide a significant advantage over other players in the same game. If your mod or addon is not listed below, use it at your own risk. If you're unsure, ask in Modmail on our [Discord server](https://ngmc.co/d).
</Info>

Unfair advantages include — but are not limited to:

- Displaying information not normally available to players, such as the distance to other players or player tracers
- Automating gameplay functions, such as key mappers, aim assists, or auto clickers
 hello 
If you see a player using prohibited modifications, report them through the NetherGames Discord bot or the [NetherGames Portal](https://ngmc.co/request).

## Resource packs and addons

<Warning>
  The following resource packs and addons are **not allowed** on NetherGames:

  - Hitboxes visible through walls
  - Hitbox Expand (expands entity hitboxes, making them easier to hit)
  - Utility HUD by Ambient
  - Better Combat Mod (AimAssist)
  - Freecam
  - Keymappers
  - Armour Hotswap
  - Minimaps that show players or entities
  - X-ray resource packs
  - Pro Placer, Better Placement, and Accurate Block Placement
</Warning>

The following resource packs and addons are **allowed**:

- ToggleSneak
- ToggleSprint
- NoHurtCam
- Fullbright
- Low Fire Effect
- Freelook (different from Freecam)
- Auto respawn
- Custom Crosshairs
- Health/Damage indicators (does not include armour damage for other players)
- Waypoints
- Minimaps without players
- Potion Effect Status

## Injection and APK clients

The following clients are **allowed** on NetherGames:

- Onix Client
- Flarial Client
- Atlas Client
- OderSo Client
- Meteor Client
- Astral Client
- Helix Client
- Latite Client
- BadTiger Client

<Warning>
  The following clients are **not allowed** on NetherGames. Using them will result in severe punishment — even if you are not actively using their cheat features while you play:

  - Any client with tools to help cheat in-game, even if unused
  - Toolbox for MCPE
  - Borion Client
  - Horion Client
  - Eternal Client
  - Zephyr Client
  - EM Client
  - Nitr0 Client
  - LiquidBounce
</Warning>
