---
title: "Linking Your NetherGames and Discord Accounts"
description: "Connect your Microsoft and Discord accounts through the NetherGames portal to verify your identity with staff and sync your in-game ranks to Discord."
---

Linking your Discord account to your NetherGames account lets staff verify who you are when you open a support ticket, and it automatically syncs your in-game ranks and tiers to your Discord profile in our server. The process takes just a few minutes through the NetherGames portal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the account portal">
    Go to [portal.nethergames.org/auth/account](https://portal.nethergames.org/auth/account) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with your Microsoft account">
    Sign in with the Microsoft (Xbox) account you use to play Minecraft. If you're already logged in, this step will be skipped automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Link Discord Account'">
    From the options shown on the account page, choose **Link Discord Account**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Follow the on-screen steps">
    Complete the remaining steps shown in the portal. You'll be prompted to authorise the connection through Discord, then your accounts will be linked.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Make sure you're signing in with the correct Microsoft account — the one associated with the Minecraft username you play on NetherGames with. Linking the wrong account won't sync your ranks correctly.
</Note>
