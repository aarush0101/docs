Skyblock crates let you spend keys to win in-game items ranging from crops and money to enchantment books and rank kits. There are four key types — Voter, Common, Rare, and Mythic — and the quality of possible rewards scales with key rarity. Each reward within a key's loot table is assigned a weight: the higher the weight, the higher the probability of receiving that item. Some quantities are randomised within set intervals that increase with key rarity.

<Info>
  Power and Lucky Shard amounts are always random within intervals defined for each key tier. The higher the key rarity, the wider the potential range.
</Info>

| 1x Rare Enchantment Book (random) | 5 |
| 8x Potato | 10 |
| 8x Carrot | 10 |
| 8x Sugarcane | 10 |
| 8x Nether Wart | 10 |
| Power Shards (random amount) | 15 |
| Lucky Shards (random amount) | 15 |
| 1x Money Pouch ($1–$1,000) | 20 |
