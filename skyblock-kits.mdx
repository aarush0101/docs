---
title: "Skyblock Kits for Ranked Players"
description: "See exactly what items are included in each Skyblock rank kit — Ultra, Emerald, and Legend — and how often ranked players can redeem them."
---

Skyblock offers daily kits to players who hold a premium rank. Each kit contains a set of items to give you a head start on your island, from basic tools and materials through to powerful combat supplies. You can redeem your kit once per day.

<Note>
  Kits are only available to ranked players. If you do not hold a rank, you will not have access to these kits.
</Note>

## Ultra kit

| Item | Quantity |
|------|----------|
| Chainmail armour (full set) | 1 set |
| Stone sword, pickaxe, axe, shovel, and hoe | 1 each |
| Water bucket | 1 |
| Lava bucket | 1 |
| Oak wood | 64 |
| Golden apples | 10 |
| Enchanted golden apple | 1 |
| Enderpearls | 2 |

## Legend kit

| Item | Quantity |
|------|----------|

<Tip>
