---
title: "Payment Options for Players in India"
description: "Understand why card payments may fail for Indian players, how to resolve international transaction blocks, and which payment methods are unavailable."
---

Players in India may encounter limited payment options when shopping on the NetherGames Store. This is largely due to how Indian banks handle international transactions, rather than a restriction imposed by NetherGames itself. If you are having trouble completing a purchase, the information below explains what is happening and what you can do.

## Why your card might be declined

When you attempt to pay on the NetherGames Store, you may see an error such as **"Card not supported"** or **"Transaction not allowed."** In most cases, this means your bank has blocked international transactions on your card.

<Note>
  Contact your bank and ask them to enable international transactions on your card. Once enabled, try your purchase again.
</Note>

## Unsupported payment methods

<Warning>
  NetherGames does not currently support UPI or RuPay. There is no timeline for when these payment methods may become available.
</Warning>

<Info>
  Google Play gift cards and other third-party gift cards are also not accepted on the NetherGames Store.
</Info>

## If enabling international transactions does not work

If contacting your bank does not resolve the issue, there is currently no alternative method to purchase items on the store from India. You may ask a friend in a supported region to purchase a gift on your behalf — see [Sending and Receiving Gifts](/sending-and-receiving-gifts) for how that process works and what geo-locking rules apply.
