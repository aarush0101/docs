---
title: "Titan Rank Perks: Frequently Asked Questions"
description: "Answers to common questions about using your Titan rank perks, including private games, nicknames, flight, replays, discounts, and billing."
---

The Titan rank comes with a range of exclusive perks that go beyond cosmetics. If you have just subscribed or you are trying to get the most out of your rank, the questions below cover the most common things players ask about using Titan features in-game and on the store.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I create a Private Game?">
    You can create a private game directly from the lobby:

    1. Open the **Social Menu** in your hotbar (the friends icon, 8th item slot).
    2. Select **Parties** > **Create Party** > **Settings** > **Private Games** > **Submit**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change my rank tag?">
    <Info>
      If you own multiple ranks, you can choose to display Ultra, Emerald, Legend, or Titan as your prefix.
    </Info>

    1. Open the **Social Menu** in your hotbar (the friends icon, 8th item slot).
    2. Select **NameTag Settings**, then select the rank you want to display.
    3. Scroll down and press **Submit** to confirm. Your chosen rank prefix will now appear in chat and above your name.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change my nickname?">
    1. Open the **Social Menu** and select **Nametag Settings**.
    2. Scroll down to **Change Your Nick** and tap or click the text box.
    3. Enter your desired nickname, then press **Submit**.

    <Warning>
      Do not use custom nicknames to impersonate staff members, partners, or other players with bad intent. Doing so may result in punishment. See the [NetherGames Guidelines](https://support.nethergames.org/enforcement-system#other:~:text=Inappropriate%20Nickname/Pet%20Name) for details.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I fly in the lobby?">
    1. Open **Profile Settings** in your hotbar (the gear icon, 9th item slot).
    2. Tap or click **Toggle Fly**, then press **Submit**.
    3. Double-jump to start flying. Double-jump again to land and disable flight.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I watch Replays?">
    1. Open **Profile Settings** in your hotbar (the gear icon, 9th item slot).
    2. Tap or click the **Replays** button.

    From there, you can search for a specific replay by its ID or browse your recent matches.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I enable my red guild tag?">
    1. Open the **Social Menu** (8th item slot) and tap or click **Guilds**.
    2. Scroll down to **Change guild tag** and tap or click the button.
    3. Choose your red guild tag and press **Submit**.

    <Warning>
      Your guild must be at least level 25 to use this feature.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the 5% Titan store discount work?">
    Your Titan rank automatically applies a 5% discount on the NetherGames store at checkout. You do not need to enter a code — as long as you are logged in with the Minecraft account that holds the Titan rank, the discount will appear when you go to pay.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When does my Titan subscription renew?">
    Your subscription renews 30 days after your original purchase date. If you purchased a 3-month, 6-month, or 1-year plan, renewal happens after that full period. Payments renew automatically unless you cancel. To cancel, see [Managing Your Titan Subscription](/managing-your-titan-subscription).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my Titan rank?">
    <Tip>
      Make sure you are logged in with the same Xbox Live account you use to play NetherGames, otherwise the cancellation option may not appear.
    </Tip>

    Visit the [NetherGames Store](https://ngmc.co/store) and click the gear icon to open your store User Settings. Navigate to the **Subscriptions** tab, locate your active Titan subscription, and click **Cancel**. For step-by-step instructions, see [Managing Your Titan Subscription](/managing-your-titan-subscription).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
