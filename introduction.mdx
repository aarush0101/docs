## Connecting to the server

NetherGames runs on **Minecraft Bedrock Edition**. You can join from mobile (iOS and Android), console (Xbox and PlayStation), and Windows. The server address is **play.nethergames.org** on port **19123**.

For a full walkthrough, see the [Connecting on Mobile & Console](/connecting-on-mobile-and-console) guide.

## Game modes and community

NetherGames offers a variety of game modes for all play styles — from competitive PvP to survival-style experiences like Factions and Skyblock. You can earn credits in-game to climb through seasonal tiers, join or create a guild with friends, and unlock cosmetics along the way.

Join our Discord community to stay up to date with announcements, participate in events, and connect with other players.

## Ranks

Premium ranks — Ultra, Emerald, Legend, and Titan — unlock exclusive perks, cosmetics, and in-game bonuses. You can browse and purchase ranks at [ngmc.co/store](https://ngmc.co/store). If you need help understanding what each rank includes, visit [ngmc.co/premium](https://ngmc.co/premium).

## Getting help

If you can't find what you need in the Help Center, there are two ways to reach our team:

- **Modmail** — Join our Discord server and message **@Modmail** for direct support. Learn more at [ngmc.co/modmail](https://ngmc.co/modmail).
- **Request form** — Submit a formal request (billing issues, player reports, appeals, and more) at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request).
