The NetherGames name, logo, and brand assets are the property of the NetherGames Network. This page explains when and how you may use them. If you need the assets themselves, download the official media kit at [ngmc.co/media](https://ngmc.co/media). For full branding details, visit [ngmc.co/branding](https://ngmc.co/branding).

## Available logo versions

There are two versions of the NetherGames logo:

- **Icon version** — features only the lava-drip icon
- **Full version** — contains the NETHERGAMES wordmark alongside the icon

Both versions are the property of the NetherGames Network.

## Permission requirements

You are prohibited from using any version of the NetherGames logo or any NetherGames brand assets without written permission from NetherGames.

To request permission, contact the support team with:

- A detailed description of the intended usage
- Which specific assets you plan to use
- A draft showing how the brand assets will be incorporated (required for visual content)

### Exception

Written permission is **not** required in the following case:

- You are using NetherGames assets in a YouTube video that promotes NetherGames

## Usage rules

Even with permission (or when using assets under the exception above), the following rules always apply:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do not modify brand assets">
    You must use the original versions of all NetherGames brand assets without alteration. For example, you may not convert the NetherGames logo to greyscale or change the orange colour to yellow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do not incorporate assets into your own branding">
    You may not integrate NetherGames brand assets into your own brand identity. For example, you may not take the lava-drip icon and add your business name to it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do not imply endorsement">
    You may not use NetherGames brand assets in a way that suggests NetherGames endorses your product, event, or service. For example, you may not place the NetherGames logo on a sign-up form for an in-game Minecraft tournament.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do not use assets in misleading or unlawful contexts">
    You may not use NetherGames brand assets in any misleading or unlawful setting. For example, you may not use the logo on publications that promote gambling, explicit content, the sale of alcohol or tobacco, or any material that violates the law.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
