If you believe a ban or mute was issued incorrectly, you can submit a formal appeal through the NetherGames support portal. An impartial staff member — separate from the one who issued the punishment — will review the evidence and decide whether the punishment stands. Because of privacy reasons, NetherGames cannot share the evidence attached to your punishment, but you can be confident it will be reviewed fairly.

<Warning>
Submitting a false appeal or attempting to evade your punishment while an appeal is pending can result in additional or permanent consequences. Only appeal if you genuinely believe the punishment was issued in error.
</Warning>

## How to submit an appeal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the support portal">
    Open [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your Xbox account">
    Log in with the Microsoft (Xbox) account you use to play Minecraft. This must be the account that received the punishment — appeals from linked accounts are not accepted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Appeals">
    Choose the **Appeals** option from the request type list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your punishment type">
    Select the type of punishment you want to appeal (for example, a ban or a mute).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out and submit the form">
    Complete all required fields and submit your appeal. You will receive a response within 7 days to the email address registered on your Xbox account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
You can check the status of your appeal at any time by visiting [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) and viewing your submissions.
</Note>

## Linked accounts

You may have received a punishment because an account linked to yours was punished. NetherGames' linked accounts system detects shared devices or accounts to prevent punishment evasion. Only the account that directly received the punishment can submit an appeal — the system does not accept appeals from linked accounts.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I can't access the account that was punished. What can I do?">
    Unfortunately, there is nothing NetherGames can do in this situation. Only the account that directly received the punishment can appeal under the current system. Staff members are not able to process appeals on behalf of other accounts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I was punished because of a linked account I don't own. What should I do?">
    The linked accounts system pairs players together when devices or accounts are shared, to prevent punishment evasion. NetherGames has no way to remove linked accounts, so it's important not to share your account or device with other players.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My appeal was denied. Can I appeal again?">
    No. If your appeal is denied, the decision is final. Staff members are not able to discuss the punishment further, as the appeal system is the last opportunity for a punishment to be reviewed.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
