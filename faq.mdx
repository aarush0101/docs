## Ranks & Store

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I buy a premium rank (Ultra, Emerald, Legend, Titan)?">
    You can find all premium ranks in our store at [ngmc.co/store](https://ngmc.co/store). For details on what each rank includes, visit [ngmc.co/premium](https://ngmc.co/premium).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How can I cancel my Titan subscription?">
    You can cancel your subscription at any time at [ngmc.co/cancel](https://ngmc.co/cancel).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My Titan subscription expired and I didn't buy Legend. What happens to my guild?">
    Your guild becomes "disabled," meaning it cannot gain further GXP until the guild leader is eligible again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I claim a Titan gift code while I already have Titan?">
    Yes — the value will be added to your subscription credit. You can view this under **User Settings > Subscriptions** at [ngmc.co/store](https://ngmc.co/store).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What stats does the Stats Transfer Key transfer?">
    The Stats Transfer Key transfers kills, deaths, wins, and losses, as stated in the store description.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do transfer keys work?">
    A transfer key is a gift code. When the target player redeems it, their stats are replaced with yours (for the fields covered by that key). Your original stats are reset to zero.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is your refund policy?">
    Refunds are only provided for unauthorized or erroneous purchases. Read the full policy at [ngmc.co/refund](https://ngmc.co/refund).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I was charged too much. Can I get a refund?">
    Yes. Submit a Billing Issue at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) and select the affected payment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My account was banned for 'Chargeback.' What should I do?">
    A chargeback occurs when you or your guardian disputed a charge with your bank. If you did not receive the service, open a Billing Issue at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request). See [ngmc.co/refund](https://ngmc.co/refund) for more details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What payment methods are supported in the store?">
    The store accepts major debit and credit card brands (cards with international purchase support), PaysafeCard, and PayPal. Google Pay, Apple Pay, and PayPal each require a valid debit or credit card issued in a supported country.

    <Note>
      International card payments are not widely available from banks in some countries (for example, India). NetherGames is working on integrating with Paytm.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My card is being declined. What should I do?">
    This is typically a bank-side issue. Enable international payments with your bank, and if the problem persists, contact your bank directly with the timestamps of your payment attempts. NetherGames cannot assist with card declines.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why can't I create new subscriptions using PayPal?">
    PayPal subscriptions have been temporarily sunset due to low demand and high maintenance costs. You can still purchase Titan gift codes using PayPal. Redeeming a gift code while you have an active Titan subscription automatically extends your end date.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My PayPal payments are stuck in a 'Pending' state. What should I do?">
    Each new PayPal payment requires manual authorization through a popup window. Make sure you're not blocking popups. If no window appears, press **Reopen Authorization Window** in the bottom-left. If issues continue, try paying by card or submit a Billing Issue at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I use Apple Pay in the store?">
    If the Apple Pay button is disabled, press or hover over it for further instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I use Google Pay in the store?">
    If the Google Pay button is disabled, press or hover over it for further instructions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why is my price not discounted when purchasing a gift?">
    Gift codes are excluded from localized pricing to prevent abuse.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer sales in the store?">
    Yes, seasonal sales are offered occasionally. These are not announced in advance — join our Discord server to stay tuned for updates.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I earn cosmetic crate keys?">
    Learn how to earn crate keys at [ngmc.co/cratekeys](https://ngmc.co/cratekeys).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
