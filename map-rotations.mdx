NetherGames rotates its map pool throughout the year to keep matches fresh and varied. Each rotation brings a new set of maps into play across all supported game modes. Below is the rotation schedule followed by a full list of currently active maps.

## Rotation schedule

| Rotation | Dates |
|----------|-------|
| Rotation 1 | January 10th – March 31st |
| Rotation 2 | April 1st – June 30th |
| Rotation 3 | July 1st – October 14th |
| Rotation 3 + Spooky Season maps | October 15th – November 9th |
| Rotation 4 | November 10th – December 9th |
| Rotation 4 + Winter Holiday maps | December 10th – January 9th |
