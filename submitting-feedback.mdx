---
title: "How to Submit Feedback and Suggestions"
description: "Share ideas and feature requests with the NetherGames team through the feedback channel on our Discord server, and upvote suggestions from other players."
---

The NetherGames team actively reads player feedback and uses it to inform development decisions. You can submit suggestions, vote on existing ideas, and add context to posts from other community members — all through the **#feedback** channel on our Discord server.

<Note>
  You need to be a member of the NetherGames Discord server to submit feedback. If you haven't joined yet, see [joining our Discord server](/joining-our-discord-server) first.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the #feedback channel">
    In the NetherGames Discord server, navigate to the **#feedback** channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for duplicates">
    Before posting, search for existing suggestions on the same topic. Duplicate posts split community support and make it harder for the team to gauge interest.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upvote or comment on duplicates">
    If a suggestion matching yours already exists, upvote it. If you have additional context or detail that strengthens the idea, leave a comment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit a new post">
    If no duplicate exists, create a new post and follow the guidelines shown to you when submitting. Complete all required fields so the team has enough context to evaluate your suggestion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Clear, specific suggestions are more likely to get a response. Describe the problem you're trying to solve, not just the solution you have in mind — this gives developers the context to implement it in the best possible way.
</Tip>
