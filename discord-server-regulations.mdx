By joining the [NetherGames Discord server](https://ngmc.co/d), you agree to follow the regulations below. Violations may result in a warning or a punishment at our discretion, depending on the nature and severity of the offence.

<Warning>
  You must be at least 13 years old to hold a Discord account. We will ban underage users in accordance with [Discord's Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms).
</Warning>

## Regulations

**1. No swearing, profanity, or discrimination**

You may not use foul language directed at a specific user or anywhere in the server. Discrimination or inappropriate comments based on race, gender, or targeting any individual are prohibited.

Offenders will be issued a warning. Depending on the severity of the offence, we may issue a permanent ban without notice.

**2. Do not unnecessarily ping or contact others**

Only ping other members — especially staff — when you have a valid reason. If you have a question, contact **@Modmail** and a staff member will reply as soon as possible.

Offenders will be issued a warning.

**3. No advertising**

You may not advertise or promote Discord servers, programs, websites, or services, including sending unsolicited invites via direct messages. An exception applies to Discord servers directly related to NetherGames, such as guild servers.

Offenders will be issued a warning.

**4. Keep personal information safe**

Do not post personally identifiable information in the server or share it with others. Our server is not a secure environment for sharing private information with unknown users.

**5. No inappropriate content**

You may not post content that is commonly considered inappropriate or that others may find offensive. Before posting something, ask yourself whether it's content you'd be comfortable sharing in a public setting — if not, don't post it.

Offenders will be issued a warning.

**6. No spamming**

You may not repeatedly post the same word or phrase to gain attention or annoy other members. This also covers chunks of nonsense text and chain messages.

Offenders will be issued a warning.

**7. No evading bans or assisting ban evaders**

<Warning>
  You may not evade your own punishment or help another user evade theirs. Offenders under this regulation are immediately banned.
</Warning>

Appropriate systems are in place to detect evasion attempts. You are obliged to respect the judgement of our staff members.

**8. Follow Discord policies**

You must comply with [Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines](https://dis.gd/tos) at all times while using the NetherGames Discord server.

## Warnings system

- At **3 warnings**, you will receive a 24-hour mute.
- At **6 warnings**, you will receive a permanent ban.

If you're unsure whether a specific behaviour is allowed, contact our support team through Modmail on our [Discord server](https://ngmc.co/d) before proceeding.

<Info>
  We reserve the right to update these rules at any time without prior notice. Changes may take effect immediately. We also reserve the right to ban, limit, or refuse service to any user at our discretion, regardless of whether the above regulations have been broken.
</Info>
