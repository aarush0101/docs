Factions crates reward you with items useful for island defence, combat, and progression. There are four key types in Factions: KOTH, Common, Rare, and Mythical. The quality and quantity of possible rewards increases with key rarity. Each item in a key's loot table has a weight assigned to it — the higher the weight, the greater the chance of receiving that reward. Some values, such as Power and Lucky Shard amounts, are randomised within intervals that scale with key rarity.

<Info>
  Power and Lucky Shard amounts are random within intervals that increase as key rarity increases.
</Info>

<Note>
  KOTH Keys are one of the four key types available in Factions but do not have a separate loot table listed here. The tables below cover Common, Rare, and Mythical keys.
</Note>
