---
title: "Buying a Premium Rank on NetherGames"
description: "Learn how to purchase an Ultra, Emerald, Legend, or Titan rank for yourself or as a gift, choose a payment method, and complete checkout."
---

NetherGames offers several premium ranks you can buy directly from the store. You can purchase a rank for your own account or generate a gift code to share with another player. Before you start, make sure you have a supported payment method ready and that you are signed in with the Xbox Live account you use to play on NetherGames.

<Note>
  If you already own Ultra or Emerald, upgrading to a higher rank is discounted — you only pay the difference as if you had bought the higher rank from the start.
</Note>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Buying for yourself">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sign in and choose a rank">
        Open the [NetherGames Store](https://ngmc.co/store) and sign in with your Xbox Live account. Browse the available ranks, then press **Add to cart** on the rank you want.

        <Note>
          Safari on iOS and iPadOS is not supported due to technical constraints. Use a different browser if you are on an Apple device.
        </Note>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open your cart">
        Once the item is in your cart, press the **Cart** button (the shiny golden button in the navigation bar) to proceed. If you want to add more items first, do that before pressing Cart.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review your order">
        In your shopping cart, confirm the items listed are correct, then press **Checkout**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose a payment method">
        In the checkout modal, select your preferred payment method. NetherGames currently supports:

        - **Credit/debit cards** — Mastercard, Visa, and American Express
        - **PayPal** — note that PayPal requires you to complete a mandatory authorization for every purchase; adding a card directly avoids this step
        - **Google Pay / Apple Pay** — available on supported devices for faster autofill of card details

        You can also apply a coupon code at this step if you have one.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete your purchase">
        Once your payment details are entered and your funds are sufficient, press **Purchase**. You will see a success or error message. If you receive an error and need clarification, contact the team via Modmail on the [NetherGames Discord](https://ngmc.co/d).
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Warning>
      NetherGames does not accept RuPay, UPI, Google Play gift cards, or other gift card types. Indian players who cannot use an international card should see the [Payment Options in India](/payment-options-in-india) page for guidance.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Buying a gift">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Sign in and choose a rank">
        Open the [NetherGames Store](https://ngmc.co/store) and sign in with your Xbox Live account. Navigate to the rank you want to gift, then press **Gift** instead of "Add to cart".
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose a payment method">
        In the checkout modal, select your preferred payment method. The same options available for personal purchases apply here: Mastercard, Visa, American Express, PayPal, Google Pay, and Apple Pay. Apply a coupon code if you have one.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete your purchase">
        Press **Purchase**. You will see a success or error message. If you need help with an error, reach out via Modmail on the [NetherGames Discord](https://ngmc.co/d).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Share your gift code">
        After a successful purchase, the settings modal will appear — you can also reopen it by clicking your username in the upper-right corner of the store. Copy the gift code URL and share it with your recipient via any platform or use it in a giveaway.

        <Note>
          Gift code URLs expire after 48 hours, but you can generate a new link for the same gift at any time.
        </Note>
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
