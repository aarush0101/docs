title: "How XP Is Allocated Across Game Modes"
description: "See exactly how much XP you earn per win, kill, participation, and other actions in every NetherGames game mode to level up your account faster."
---

XP is awarded when you play certain game modes on NetherGames. The more XP you accumulate, the higher your level on the network becomes. You can check how much XP each level requires at [ngmc.co/xp](https://ngmc.co/xp).

The tables below show the XP you can earn in each game mode and what actions grant it.

## Bedwars

| Mode | Win | Kill | Participation | Final kill | Bed |
|------|-----|------|---------------|------------|-----|
| Solo | 9 XP | 1 XP | 1 XP | 3 XP | 5 XP |
| Doubles | 9 XP | 1 XP | 1 XP | 3 XP | 5 XP |
| Squads | 9 XP | 1 XP | 1 XP | 3 XP | 5 XP |
| 1v1 | 9 XP | 1 XP | 1 XP | 3 XP | 5 XP |
| 2v2 | 9 XP | 1 XP | 1 XP | 3 XP | 5 XP |

<Info>
In Bedwars, Skywars, and Survival Games, kill XP scales with your kill count per game: 1 XP for 1 kill, 2 XP for 3 kills, and 3 XP for 6 kills. 3 XP is the maximum you can receive from kills in these game modes.
