The NetherGames Store lets you buy items as gifts for other players and accept gifts sent to you. Gifts are a great way to support a friend or run a giveaway, but there are important geo-locking rules you need to understand before purchasing — especially if you are buying at a regionally discounted price.

## Gift rules and geo-locking

NetherGames offers reduced regional pricing in certain countries to make items more affordable. If you buy a gift at a reduced regional price, the gift can only be redeemed by players in specific countries. The table below summarises which currencies unlock gifts for which regions.

| Purchase currency | Who can redeem the gift |
|-------------------|------------------------|
| USD, AUD, CAD, EUR, GBP | Players in any country |
| PLN, BRL, MXN | Players in Poland, Brazil, Mexico, India, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Pakistan, and Turkey |
| INR, BDT, IDR, LKR, MYR | Players in India, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, Malaysia, Pakistan, and Turkey |
| PKR, TRY | Players in Pakistan and Turkey only |

<Warning>
  NetherGames can detect VPN usage. Attempting to use a VPN to bypass geo-locking restrictions will not work, and you will not qualify for a refund if you try. If a gift cannot be redeemed due to geo-locking rules, support cannot override this.
</Warning>

Once a gift has been redeemed by the recipient, it is no longer eligible for a refund within the change-of-mind refund period. This policy exists to prevent gift scamming. See the [Refund Policy](https://support.nethergames.org/localised-pricing) for full details.

## Purchasing a gift

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose an item on the store">
    Open the [NetherGames Store](https://store.nethergames.org) and navigate to the item you want to gift.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the Gift option">
    Press **Gift**, which appears to the left of the "Add to cart" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    Select your payment method, review your order, and confirm the purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your Gift Inventory">
    After purchasing, click the gift icon in the top-right corner of the store to open your **Gift Inventory**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate a gift link">
    Expand the entry for the gift you just purchased and click **Generate Link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the link">
    Copy the link and send it to your recipient directly, or use it in a giveaway.

    <Note>
      Gift links expire after 48 hours. If your link expires before it is redeemed, you can generate a new one from your Gift Inventory at any time.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Redeeming a gift

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the gift link">
    Click the link shared with you by the sender.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the gift">
    Press **Accept** on the page that opens. The item will be added to your account immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you experience any issues sending or receiving gifts, contact the support team by sending a direct message to the ModMail bot on the [NetherGames Discord](https://discord.gg/UFQfD6ThVF).
