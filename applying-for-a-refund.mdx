---
title: "Applying for a Refund on NetherGames"
description: "Find out how to submit a refund request for an eligible NetherGames purchase, what to expect after submitting, and key limitations to know."
---

If your purchase qualifies for a refund under the [NetherGames Refund Policy](https://support.nethergames.org/refund-policy), you can submit a request directly through the support portal. Make sure you review the refund policy before submitting to confirm your purchase is eligible — not all transactions qualify.

<Warning>
  NetherGames cannot provide refunds for any purchases made with PaysafeCard.
</Warning>

## Submitting a refund request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the support portal">
    Visit [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your Microsoft account">
    Log in with the Microsoft (Xbox) account you use to play Minecraft, if you are not already signed in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the ticket type">
    Choose **Billing Issue** from the available options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your transaction">
    Use the dropdown menus to select the transaction you want refunded and the payment method used for that purchase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explain your reason">
    Fill in the reason why you are requesting a refund. Be as clear as possible to help the team process your request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and wait for a response">
    Submit your request. You will receive a response within 7 days to the email address registered on your Xbox account. You can also check the status of your submission at any time by returning to [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) and viewing your submissions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If your refund is approved, it generally takes 5–10 business days for the funds to return to your account, depending on your payment provider.
</Warning>
