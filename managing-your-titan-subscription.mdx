---
title: "Managing Your Titan Subscription and Transfers"
description: "Step-by-step instructions for cancelling your Titan rank subscription or transferring remaining days to another NetherGames player."
---

Your Titan subscription renews automatically each billing period until you cancel it. You can also transfer any remaining paid days to another player if you no longer want the rank on your account. Both actions are handled directly on the NetherGames Store, and neither requires you to contact support unless something goes wrong.

## Cancelling your subscription

When you cancel, you will not be charged again, but your Titan perks will remain active until the current subscription period ends.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the NetherGames Store">
    Visit [store.nethergames.org](https://store.nethergames.org).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your Microsoft account">
    Click the login button in the top-right corner and sign in with the Microsoft account linked to your Minecraft gamertag.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your account settings">
    Once signed in, click your username in the top-right corner to open your account settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your subscription">
    Under the **Subscriptions** category, locate your active Titan subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel the subscription">
    Click the **Cancel** button. Your subscription will stop renewing and your perks will continue until the current period expires.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Transferring your remaining Titan days

You can transfer the days remaining on your Titan rank to another player, provided you are not banned. If your Titan rank comes from an active subscription, the subscription will automatically cancel when you initiate the transfer, and the recipient will receive the remaining paid days.

For full details, refer to the [Refund Policy](https://support.nethergames.org/refund-policy).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the NetherGames Store">
    Visit [store.nethergames.org](https://store.nethergames.org).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your Microsoft account">
    Click the login button in the top-right corner and sign in with the Microsoft account linked to your Minecraft gamertag.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open your account settings">
    Once signed in, click your username in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your subscription">
    Under the **Subscriptions** category, locate your active Titan subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate the transfer">
    Click the **arrow** button on your subscription, enter the recipient's username, and press **Continue**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Recipient accepts the transfer">
    The recipient will need to visit [store.nethergames.org](https://store.nethergames.org) to accept the Titan rank. If you change your mind before they accept, open your **Gift Inventory** and click the **X** button to cancel the transfer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  If you run into problems managing your subscription, contact the support team in the **#support** channel on [Discord](https://ngmc.co/discord) or open a Billing Issue ticket at [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request).
</Info>
