---
title: "How to Apply for a Staff Role at NetherGames"
description: "Learn the eligibility requirements and step-by-step process for submitting a volunteer staff application on the NetherGames Network."
---

NetherGames accepts applications for staff roles through the NetherGames Portal. Before you apply, check the [vacancies page](https://ngmc.co/vacancies) to review the requirements for the specific role you're interested in — requirements vary by position.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the application portal">
    Visit [ngmc.co/jobs](https://ngmc.co/jobs) to open the NetherGames Portal request page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your Microsoft account">
    Log in with the Microsoft (Xbox) account you use to play Minecraft. If you're already signed in, this step is automatic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select 'Apply for Staff'">
    Choose **Apply for Staff** from the list of available request types.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your desired role">
    Select the type of staff role you would like to apply for.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete and submit the application">
    Fill out all required fields in the application form, then submit it. Be as detailed and honest as possible in your responses.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once submitted, you will receive a response within **7 days** to the email address registered on your Xbox account. You can check your application status at any time by visiting your submissions at [ngmc.co/jobs](https://ngmc.co/jobs).

<Warning>
  Do not message staff members asking about the status of a pending application. If more than 7 days have passed without a response, contact us through Modmail on our Discord server at [ngmc.co/d](https://ngmc.co/d).
</Warning>
