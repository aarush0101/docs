Credits and tiers are NetherGames' yearly reward system, giving you a path to earn perks just by playing — no purchase required. Each season runs from July through June, and your credit total determines which tier you reach. Higher tiers unlock chat colours, XP boosts, pets, replays, and more.

<Info>
  A new tiers season started on 1 July 2025. The next season is scheduled to begin on 1 July 2026.
</Info>

## Tiers available

Reaching a credit threshold permanently unlocks that tier's perks for the rest of the season.

| Tier | Credits required |
|------|-----------------|
| Steel | 2,500 |
| Bronze | 5,000 |
| Silver | 10,000 |
| Gold | 20,000 |
| Opal | 40,000 |
| Amethyst | 80,000 |
| Sapphire | 120,000 |
| Diamond | 200,000 |

## Credit allocation by game mode

The tables below show exactly how many credits each action earns you in each mode.

### Bedwars

| Mode | Bed broken | Final kill | Win | Perfect win |
|------|-----------|-----------|-----|-------------|
| Solos | 5 | 2 | 7 | 3 |
| Doubles | 7 | 3 | 9 | 5 |
| Squads | 9 | 3 | 11 | 8 |
| Duels | 2 | 1 | 3 | N/A |

<Info>
  A *Perfect Game* is one where over 75% of the game is played with a full lobby. You earn a *Perfect Win* by winning the match, breaking every other team's bed, and getting the final kill on every other player. Perfect Win credits are a bonus on top of normal win credits — for example, a perfect Solos win gives you 7 (win) + 3 (perfect win) = 10 credits.
</Info>
