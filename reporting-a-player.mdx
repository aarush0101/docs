---
title: "How to Report a Player on NetherGames"
description: "Submit a player report for in-game rule violations or Discord misconduct through the NetherGames support portal, with video evidence to support your case."
---

If you see a player breaking the rules — whether in-game or on the NetherGames Discord server — you can submit a formal report through the support portal. Before you report, make sure you're familiar with what counts as a rule violation. Review the [enforcement system](/enforcement-system) and the [Discord server regulations](/discord-server-regulations) so your report covers a genuine breach.

<Note>
Video evidence significantly strengthens your report. Reports without supporting evidence may be difficult to act on, so record the behaviour before submitting wherever possible.
</Note>

## How to submit a report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the support portal">
    Open [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your Xbox account">
    Log in with the Microsoft (Xbox) account you use to play Minecraft.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your report type">
    Select **Player Report** to report a rule violation in-game, or **Discord Report** to report a user on the NetherGames Discord server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out and submit the form">
    Complete all required fields, attach your evidence, and submit the report.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once submitted, you will receive an update within 7 days to the email address registered on your Xbox account. You can also check the status of your report at any time by visiting [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) and viewing your submissions.

<Warning>
Do not message staff members asking about the status of your report while it is still pending. If more than 7 days pass without a response, contact the team through Modmail on the NetherGames Discord server, or use the live chat at the bottom of [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request).
</Warning>
