Boosting the NetherGames Discord server with Discord Nitro unlocks a set of exclusive perks as a thank you for your support. All rewards are applied to your account automatically as soon as you boost — there's nothing extra you need to do.

## Booster perks

When you boost the NetherGames Discord server, you receive all of the following:

- **External emojis and stickers** — Use emojis and stickers from other Discord servers anywhere in our server.
- **Access to #boosters** — An exclusive Discord channel available only to active boosters.
- **Evolving boosting badge** — A badge on your Discord profile that evolves the longer you continue to boost.
- **Nickname permission** — The ability to change your own nickname in the server.

<Note>
  Perks are tied to your active boost. If you remove your boost, the perks will be revoked automatically.
</Note>
