---
title: "Fix 'This Username Is Already Registered' Error"
description: "Why you see the 'username already registered' error when joining NetherGames and how to resolve it depending on your specific situation."
---

If you receive an error saying your username is already registered when trying to join NetherGames, it means the Xbox Gamertag you're using has been used to join the network before. This page explains the possible causes and how to fix each one.

## Why this happens

This error occurs when a NetherGames account already exists for your Xbox Gamertag. NetherGames may rename or remove accounts that have been inactive for at least a year, but if the existing account still exists, you will need to resolve the conflict before you can join.

## Identify your situation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search for your username on the portal">
    Open the [NetherGames Portal](https://portal.nethergames.org) and search for your username.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check whether the profile is yours">
    - If the profile belongs to you (you've joined NetherGames before), continue to **Solution A** or **Solution B** below.
    - If you haven't joined before, or the profile doesn't belong to you, go to **Solution C**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Solution A: Keep your old profile">
    Use this if you want to keep the existing NetherGames account linked to your previous Xbox account.

    1. Sign into Minecraft using your **old** Xbox account.
    2. Connect to the server at **play.nethergames.org**. This updates the profile to reflect your current Gamertag.
    3. Sign out of Minecraft.
    4. Join NetherGames on your new account.

    <Warning>
      NetherGames cannot transfer data between separate accounts. Stats, friends, islands, ranks, and other data are non-transferable. Some stats can be moved by purchasing a Stats Transfer Key.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Solution B: Delete your old profile">
    Use this if you no longer need the old account and want to free up the username.

    1. Open the [NetherGames Portal](https://portal.nethergames.org).
    2. Press **Login** in the upper-right corner.
    3. Sign in with the Microsoft credentials for your old account.
    4. Go to **Requests** > **Account Deletion**.
    5. Allow up to 48 hours for the request to be processed.
    6. Join NetherGames once you receive a confirmation email.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Solution C: The account isn't yours">
    Use this if you have never joined NetherGames before, or the existing profile does not belong to you.

    1. Open the [NetherGames Portal](https://portal.nethergames.org).
    2. Search for your username.
    3. Check when the player was last seen.

    If the account has been inactive for **over a year**, contact us through Modmail on our Discord server at [ngmc.co/discord](https://ngmc.co/discord). If the account was active recently, we are unable to assist further.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
