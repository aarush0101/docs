The NetherGames Network ("NetherGames," "we," "us," "our") collects data from its members and users in order to provide its services. Your privacy and the protection of your data are important to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over it.

## 1. Data collection

Data collected from players on our Minecraft server network is stored in our database, located in Vint Hill, United States, and hosted by OVH US LLC.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Data collected from all players">
    - Your username
    - Your email address
    - Your IP address
    - Your Minecraft client ID (a numeric string identifying your device's Minecraft application)
    - Your UUID (a unique identifier generated by the server to identify you)
    - Your XUID (a unique identifier generated by Xbox to identify you)
    - Your DID (a unique identifier generated by Minecraft to identify your device)
    - In-game data — including kills, deaths, wins, XP or levels, Credits, first and last join date and time, Minecraft client version, and device type and version
    - Your actions or actions relating to you — such as who you kill, how you die, when and why you were punished, what you post on the forums, and when you register or log in
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Additional data collected from staff members">
    - Full name
    - Date of birth
    - Staff member's signature
    - Parent or guardian signature and email address (if under 18)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Data collected when you make a purchase">
    When you purchase a product through our online store at store.nethergames.org, Stripe and PayPal collect the following on our behalf:

    - Your name
    - Your email address
    - Your bank account or credit/debit card details
    - Your physical address
    - Your IP address at the time of purchase
    - Your payment or transaction ID

    Your bank account and credit card details are **not** passed to our servers or saved by us at any time. We may log your name, email, address, and IP if you initiate a dispute or chargeback.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Data collected when logging in via Xbox Live">
    When you log in to our web portals through our authentication service, the following is collected from Xbox Live for authentication purposes only:

    - Your Xbox Live Gamertag
    - Your XUID
    - Your email address
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Data collected when verifying your Discord account">
    When you verify your Discord account through the account portal at portal.nethergames.org, the following is collected from Discord for background checks only:

    - Your Discord account ID (username and discriminator)
    - Your linked email address and email verification status
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Data collected when applying for a YouTube rank">
    When you apply for a YouTube channel rank through the portal, the following is collected from YouTube for background checks only:

    - Your YouTube channel ID
    - Hidden subscriber status
    - Subscriber count
    - Video and view counts
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## 2. Data usage

Stripe and PayPal provide us with your full name, email, and address, which we log on our servers to verify purchases. We may also use this information to resolve disputes made through Stripe, PayPal, your bank or card provider, or in a court of law.

Email addresses collected by NetherGames are used to send the following types of communications:

- **Purchase receipt emails** — sent when you buy a product from our store, including what you purchased and a transaction ID
- **News and promotional emails** — sent to tell you about new products, community updates, or other news. You can opt out at any time using the link included in these emails
- **Important announcement emails** — sent when we have an announcement that we are legally required to share with users, such as in the event of a data breach. These emails cannot be opted out of

## 3. GDPR compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), which covers all users in the European Economic Area (EEA). Under the GDPR, you can request to erase or anonymise your data from our storage systems. See Section 7 of this policy for instructions on how to do so.

## 4. Data protection

NetherGames takes appropriate steps to protect your data. These include restricting access to sensitive information — such as IP and email addresses — to trusted staff members who have signed NDAs, and securing our servers with strong security checks and firewalls.

## 5. Age of consent

To use NetherGames services, you must be at least the age of majority in your state or province of residence. Alternatively, if you have reached the age of majority, you may give consent for your minor dependents to use our services.

## 6. How we handle your data

NetherGames respects the privacy of all users by committing to the following:

- We identify the purposes for which information is collected before or at the time of collection, as outlined in this policy
- We collect and use personal information only to fulfil those stated purposes, or compatible purposes, unless we obtain your consent or are required to do so by law
- We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil those purposes
- We collect personal information by lawful and fair means, with your knowledge or consent where appropriate
- We keep personal data accurate, complete, and up to date as needed for its intended use
- We protect personal information against loss, theft, and unauthorised access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification
- We make information about our data management policies readily available to users

## 7. Requesting removal or access to your data

You can request to have your account information wiped from our database at any time. To do so, visit [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) and select the **Account Deletion** option.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit the account deletion form">
    Go to [ngmc.co/request](https://ngmc.co/request) and select **Account Deletion**. Fill in and submit the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait 14 days">
    There is a 14-day waiting period after submission in case you change your mind. Your account will not be deleted during this time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your account is permanently deleted">
    After 14 days, your account and all associated data are permanently erased from our servers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Punishment data is the only exception — it is retained even after account deletion, as it is essential to protecting our servers and community.
</Note>

## 8. Changes to this policy

NetherGames reserves the right to modify this policy without prior notice. We recommend checking back from time to time. That said, we take reasonable steps to notify users of significant changes to policies or terms.

If you have questions about your data, its collection, or your privacy, contact us via Modmail on our [Discord server](https://ngmc.co/d).
