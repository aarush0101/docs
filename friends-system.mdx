---
title: "Friends System: Add and Manage Friends"
description: "Learn how to send, accept, and manage friend requests on NetherGames, including friend slot limits by rank and how to access the Social Menu."
---

The friends system lets you track which of your friends are online across the NetherGames network. You can see when they log on, find them quickly through the Social Menu, and send direct messages without needing to know which game server they are on.

## Friend slot limits

By default, you can add up to 10 friends. Higher ranks increase that limit:

| Rank | Friend slots |
|------|-------------|
| Default | 10 |
| Ultra | 15 |
| Emerald | 25 |
| Legend & Titan | 50 |

<Info>
You can purchase extra friend slots on the [NetherGames store](https://store.nethergames.org).
</Info>

## Sending a friend request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Social Menu">
    In any lobby, find the Social Menu item in your hotbar (second-to-last slot) and tap or click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Friends">
    Click **Friends** in the Social Menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a friend">
    Click the **Add a Friend** button, then use the form to search for and select the player you want to add.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can also hit another player directly with the Social Menu item — a prompt to send them a friend request will appear immediately.

<Note>
You cannot send a friend request to a player who has blocked incoming requests. Ask them to allow friend requests in their Profile Settings if you want to add them.
</Note>

## Accepting a friend request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Social Menu">
    In any lobby, tap or click the Social Menu item in your hotbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Friend Requests">
    Click the **Friend Requests** button to see pending requests.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept the request">
    Click the request you want to accept. To decline, simply ignore it — pending requests are cleared the next time you log in.
  </Step>
</Steps>
