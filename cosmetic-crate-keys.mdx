Cosmetic crate keys let you unlock cosmetic items on the NetherGames network. These include coloured armour for the lobby, knife skins for Murder Mystery, arrow trails, kill effects, particles, trails, win effects, and more. Cosmetic crate keys are separate from Factions crate keys and work differently.

<Warning>
  This article covers cosmetic crate keys only. Factions crate keys are a separate system and are earned in a different way.
</Warning>

## Earning keys

You can earn cosmetic crate keys by winning games across most game modes. Each win gives you roughly a **5% chance** of receiving a key. Keys do not drop in Creative, Skyblock, or Factions.

If you win a key, you'll see a notification in the **Experience Summary** message that appears after your game ends.

## Redeeming keys

To open your crates, find the **chest in the lobby** and click on it to claim your prize. Each key gives you one random cosmetic from the available pool.

<Note>
  There is no cosmetic rarity — all keys have the same chance of dropping any cosmetic. You cannot receive a duplicate cosmetic. Once you've collected every available cosmetic, you won't be able to open crates until new cosmetics are added to the server.
</Note>

## Available cosmetic types

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Trails">
    Trails display a particle effect behind you while you're running. The following particles are available:

    Flame, Redstone, Heart, Enchantment, Water Drip, Lava Drip, Angry Villager, Happy Villager, Portal, Smoke, Rainbow
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Arrow trails">
    Arrow trails add a particle effect behind arrows you fire. The following particles are available:

    Flame, Redstone, Heart, Enchantment, Water Drip, Lava Drip, Angry Villager, Happy Villager, Portal, Smoke, Rainbow
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Kill effects">
    Kill effects play whenever you eliminate a player in any minigame. The following effects are available:

    Sphere Firework, Star Firework, Creeper Firework, Burst Firework, Lightning, Explosion
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Win effects">
    Win effects play whenever you win any minigame. The following effects are available:

    Sphere Firework, Star Firework, Creeper Firework, Burst Firework, Lightning, Explosion
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Particles">
    Particles are generic effects that surround you in the lobby. The following particles are available:

    Flame, Redstone, Heart, Enchantment, Water Drip, Lava Drip, Angry Villager, Happy Villager, Portal, Smoke, Rainbow
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wings">
    Wings are particle effects that form wings behind you in the lobby. The following wings are available:

    Flame, Redstone, Heart, Enchantment, Water Drip, Lava Drip, Angry Villager, Happy Villager, Portal, Smoke, Rainbow
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Coloured armour (lobby)">
    Coloured armour uses vanilla Minecraft leather dye colours plus rainbow. Each colour must be obtained separately per armour piece: Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, and Boots. This armour is visible in the lobby only.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Knife (Murder Mystery)">
    Knife skins are exclusive to Murder Mystery and let you choose your knife type when you're the murderer. The following types are available:

    Wooden, Stone, Iron, Gold, Diamond
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bed break effects (Bedwars)">
    Bed break effects trigger when you destroy an opponent's bed in Bedwars. The following effects are available:

    Sphere Firework, Star Firework, Creeper Firework, Burst Firework, Lightning, Explosion
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cage (Skywars and The Bridge)">
    Cages are the cosmetic cage you spawn in at the start of a Skywars or The Bridge game. Available colours (glass block types) include:

    White, Orange, Magenta, Light Blue, Yellow, Lime, Pink, Gray, Light Gray, Cyan, Purple, Blue, Brown, Green, Red, Black, Rainbow, Witch Pot
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Capes">
    Capes are worn on your player's back throughout the server. The following capes can be obtained from crate keys:

    Claw, Cookies, Creeper 1, Creeper 2, Creeper 3, Fox, Golem, Optifine, Sword, Mage, Shulker, Pirate

    <Note>
      Additional capes exist that are not available through crate keys. These can only be obtained by reaching certain levels, participating in events, or being a staff member.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Kill sounds">
    Kill sounds play to all nearby players when you eliminate someone in a game. The following sounds are available:

    Orb Ding, Level Up Ding, Bell Ding, Classic Pop, Modest Pop, Explosion, Hurt, Anvil Crash, Firework Blast, Large Firework Blast, Fizz
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bed break sounds (Bedwars)">
    Bed break sounds play when you destroy an opponent's bed in Bedwars. The following sounds are available:

    Orb Ding, Level Up Ding, Bell Ding, Classic Pop, Modest Pop, Explosion, Hurt, Anvil Crash, Firework Blast, Large Firework Blast, Fizz
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  Shopkeepers (Bedwars) and Conquest Flags are listed as cosmetic types in-game but are **not obtainable through crate keys**.
</Info>
