title: "NetherGames Terms of Service & Community Rules"
description: "The rules and expectations that apply to every player on NetherGames. Read these before you play so you know what's allowed and what isn't."
---

Playing on NetherGames means agreeing to follow these community rules. They exist so every player can have a positive, fair experience. Failing to follow them will result in punishments — including temporary or permanent bans. If you're unsure whether something is allowed, reach out to our team on Modmail before you do it.

<Warning>
  Be careful with sharing access to your device. Players sharing the same device are treated as linked accounts, and punishments apply to all of them. We may also consider your IP address when enforcing punishments.
</Warning>

## 1. Respect yourself and others

Do not swear at, curse at, or abuse other players. Treat others as you would like to be treated. Bullying is not tolerated at NetherGames, and offenders will be dealt with accordingly.

## 2. Keep your content appropriate

Do not post inappropriate content. A useful test: would you be comfortable showing it to your parents? This includes anything others could find offensive. Racism, sexism, and any other form of discrimination are strictly prohibited.

## 3. Be considerate while chatting

Think about what other players will see in chat before you post. Do not spam — this includes repeating the same word or phrase, or using excessive caps to attract attention or annoy others. A chat filter is in place for a reason. Profanity is permitted to a degree, but you must censor it appropriately and never direct it at another player.

## 4. Listen to and respect our staff members

Our staff members are volunteers who are here to keep the server safe and enjoyable for everyone. When they give you instructions, follow them. Engage with staff in a polite manner — they are not your servants.

## 5. No advertising or endorsing other servers

Advertising or endorsing another Minecraft Bedrock Edition server is strictly prohibited. You may mention another server for context in conversation, but any further discussion promoting it will be treated as advertising.

## 6. Keep your personal information safe

Keep personal information to yourself. Everything you send in chat is posted on a public forum and may remain there permanently. This is the internet — stay safe.

## 7. Turn off prohibited client modifications

Client modifications or "cracked clients" that are not officially endorsed by Minecraft cannot be used on NetherGames. Prohibited modifications grant unfair abilities — such as fly hacking, jump or speed boosts, or auto-clicking. Resource and texture packs that only make cosmetic changes without affecting gameplay are allowed.

<Note>
  These clients and modifications are also prohibited from running in the background while you play on NetherGames. Learn more on the [Allowed Modifications](/allowed-modifications) page.
</Note>

## 8. Use an appropriate username and skin

Your username and skin must be appropriate for players of all ages. Prohibited skins include those that use custom geometry outside the standard "Alex" or "Steve" structure, or those that show excessive body parts, as these can provide a visual advantage.

## 9. Be thoughtful of your teammates and other players

You are placed in a team with other players — trolling, griefing, or killing your own teammates is not acceptable. Teaming with players from other teams in a solo match is not allowed. On Factions, teleport trapping other players is not permitted.

## 10. Keep your click rate at an acceptable level

There is a cap of 15 clicks per second. While there are no restrictions on specific clicking methods (butterfly, drag, jitter), hardware or software that enables you to exceed this cap is prohibited. Help us keep the game fair for both mobile and desktop players.

## 11. Report bugs instead of abusing them

Abusing bugs for your advantage is prohibited, even if you have already reported them. If you find a game-breaking bug, report it to **#bugs** in our [Discord server](https://ngmc.co/d) immediately and report any player you see exploiting it.

## 12. Accept your punishment

Attempting to evade your punishment — or helping another player evade theirs — is strictly prohibited. By using NetherGames, you agree to follow these guidelines and accept consequences if you break them.

## 13. No soliciting real-money transactions on our platforms

You may not solicit financial transactions in exchange for in-game items or services, whether in server chat or on our Discord. This includes gift codes or other items with real-world economic value. Scamming in general is done at your own risk. Players found actively participating in such activities in-game will receive a 1-month ban.

## 14. Do not obstruct staff from doing their job

Do not interfere with staff members carrying out their duties. This includes — but is not limited to — telling other players that a staff member is spectating or online, or falsifying evidence.

---

## A note on changes and enforcement

NetherGames reserves the right to change these rules at any time without prior notice, and changes may take effect immediately. We also reserve the right to ban, refuse service to, or limit service access for any player at our discretion, regardless of whether they have broken the rules listed above.

If you need clarification on what is or isn't allowed, contact our support team via Modmail on our [Discord server](https://ngmc.co/d). We'd much rather answer a question than issue a punishment.
